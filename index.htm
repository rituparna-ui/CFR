About CFR.

Charity for Revolution is a Solapur based social venture which is a connecting link between the NGOs and the people of Solapur city. We help people reach these NGOs and donate money, old clothes, shoes, stationery, and any other stuff which can be reused. We also collect donations and fulfill the needs of the children living in slum areas who cannot afford anything and also look after their educational needs. .

As our name Charity for Revolution signifies the word "revolution", our prime objective is to bring revolution through the medium of charity for the underprivileged not only by donating them but also making them independent and helping them to find a medium to earn their living. .

Objectives Of CFR

To stand out as a social venture and work on a non-profit basis to supportand promote local NGO's

To help the local businesses connect with NGOs and show them the ways of helpingthe NGOsand the trusts in the city

To act as a medium to address global and local issues related to public welfare

To have youth empowerment and education as our priorities

To carry our activities and drives planned by our volunteers in our city which would contribue to change and revolution

To spread optimism and mould our public to have a humanitarian mindset
